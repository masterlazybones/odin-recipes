Bhel Puri

Description

Bhel Puri is a popular Mumbai street food where crispy puffed rice is tossed with sweet tamarind chutney, spicy green chutney, hot pungent garlic chutney, onions, tomatoes, roasted peanuts, sev and chaat masala. Lastly, it is garnished with coriander leaves, crunchy sev and crushed papdis. (source: IndianHealthyRecipes)

Ingredients

  • Mint Coriander chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chilli Garlic chutney
  • Puffed rice
  • Onions (chopped)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Green chillies (chopped)
  • Coriander leaves
  • Crushed papdis, sev and roasted peanuts (optional)

Author's Notes

I prefer my bhel without peanuts but it is entirely upto the cook to decide what goes in. Additionally, extra ingredients can also be added to your bhel (chopped unripe mangoes for example!) to add interesting flavours

Steps

  1. To the puffed rice, add all the chutneys first and give it a gentle stir.
  2. Add all the chopped vegetables and extra ingredients to this mixture.
  3. Stir until all the ingredients have mixed completely with the puffed rice.
  4. Serve your prepared Bhel in a bowl and enjoy!
